ACCOMMODATION

ROOM ONLY

These 2, 3, 4 & 6 berth rooms offer 1, 2 or 3 room configurations with combinations of single, double and twin beds and do not feature kitchens. All of these rooms are in the Oyster Bay area.

SELF CATERING APARTMENT STANDARD

These 4 and 6 berth apartments feature a Studio Apartment layout with combination of single, double or twin and feature kitchens with fridge. 4 berth apartments have one separate bedroom and 6 berths have 2. There is a double bed in the living area, and twins, single in bedroom.

SELF CATERING APARTMENT SILVER

These 4, 6 and 7 berth apartments feature 2 or 3 bedroom configurations with double and twin beds and feature kitchens with oven, microwave, fridge and kettle. All 4 berth apartments have one double bedroom and one twin bedroom and all 6 and 7 berth rooms have a double, twin and adult bunkbeds bedrooms. All of these apartments are in the Oyster Bay area.

SELF CATERING APARTMENT GOLD/SEASIDE

These 4 and 6 berth apartments feature 2 or 3 bedroom configurations with double or twin beds and feature kitchens with fridge, microwave and free towels. All 4 berth apartments have one double and one twin room and all 6 berth rooms have a double and two twin bedrooms.

DELUXE SUITES

If a room’s not quite enough but a kitchen’s too much, these suites are the perfect in-between with extra space for relaxing. These One bedroom suites with double or twin beds have Bathroom with shower over bath and complimentary toiletries, lounge area with TV. These are split on two floors with lounge area downstairs and bedroom upstairs. All suites come with towels included, free Essential WiFi, tea and coffee making facilities (tea and coffee is not provided), and a mini fridge.

HOTEL

These 2 and 4 berth hotels feature 1 or 2 bedrooms with double, twin beds or adult bunk beds. All rooms have tea making facilities, chiller and free towels. These hotel rooms are in the Ocean Hotel, Shoreline Hotel and Wave Hotel. All of these hotels are of the same high standard and are equidistant from the music arena. not all Hotel rooms will have a door separating the two areas.

HOTEL SELF CATERING PLATINUM

These 4 and 6 berth self catering apartments in the Wave Hotel feature 2 or 3 bedroom configurations with double, adult bunk and twin beds. They have full kitchen facilities including microwave and fridge and free towels.

TRAVEL

We know that once you've set off on your break with us you'll want to start having fun on resort as quickly as possible. To make this easier for you to plan your journey to Bognor Regis we've got lots of information below, including a route planner.

BY CAR

It's easy to get to Bognor Regis by car, with the resort just over an hour away from the M25 London orbital motorway and London Gatwick International Airport. We urge as many people as possible to car-share with others coming to the event. This is a sold out event and we want try to make sure everyone can park on site.

BY TRAIN

Bognor Regis is roughly an hour away from London Victoria to Bognor Regis train Station. Some services require that you change at Barnham, so be sure to check with National Rail for times and fares. Bognor Regis Railway Station is very close to the town centre, and taxis can be hired from within Bognor Regis Train Station, they cost less than £5.

If arriving by train, please enter the Butlins site via the pedestrian gate at this postcode: PO21 1NU

BY BUS

The local bus service Stagecoach, run regular services from most towns and places surrounding the Bognor Regis area, you can view timetables and fares online here.

BY COACH

National Express run services that stop at our resort entrance, for more information from their website click here.

BY FERRY

The closest ferry port to Bognor Regis is Portsmouth Harbour which is around 24 miles away, with the next closest port at Newhaven, approximately 42 miles away from resort.

BY PLANE

Gatwick Airport is the nearest international airport, which is about an hour's drive from Bognor Regis, and has a regular train service that runs from the airport to Bognor Regis. Next closest airports are Bournemouth Airport at around 67 miles away from Bognor Regis and Southampton Airport which is only 38 miles away. Also Visit Southampton Airport Guide for comprehensive travel information.
