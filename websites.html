Process

Websites are a tool to communicate.
They fail when they’re not saying the right things.

That’s why I run a content driven process to deliver websites that actually engage your audience and give you a launch-pad for your wider brand.

Step one

Goal definition

Together, we define who your target audience is, how they convert through your website and how we will measure success to ensure a laser focus on what your business needs to achieve

Step two

Brand clarity

Using a seven point story framework popularised by Disney, you become an essential guide in the story of your customers lives by focusing on their journey, not your demands

Step three

Design

Starting from the structure and moving through how the website works and eventually to how it looks and moves, an iterative process to design involves your domain expertise every step of the way

Step four

Development

Finally, the website is developed to work perfectly across all devices, load quickly, animate seamlessly and be easy for you to update using the latest tools such as Webflow
